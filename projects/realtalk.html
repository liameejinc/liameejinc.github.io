Real Talk
PROVIDING A SAFE OUTLET FOR TEENS
TO SHARE THEIR STORIES
AND ACCESS ESSENTIAL RESOURCES

Real Talk crowdsources authentic teen
stories on stigmatized topics such as
identity, relationships, and mental
health. We believe stories have the
power to foster connections and challenge
common stigmas, while simultaneously
supplying valuable resources that equips
them with the skills and knowledge they
need in order to engage better in their
relationships, school, and beyond. Real
Talk is able to provide the online community
where teens can feel comfortable sharing
their vulnerable stories, validating their
peers, and accessing helpful resources.
